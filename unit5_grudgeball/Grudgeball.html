Unit 5 · Sections 5.1-5.3 · Category Spinner Mode
  • Each team starts with 10 points.
  • Choose a category tile and the game draws a random unused question from that category.
  • A correct answer earns one seeded spinner result worth 1-3 points.
  • Protect results add points to the answering team. Attack results subtract points from a selected opponent.
  • Wrong answers can be diverted to another team.
  • The team with the most points after all questions are used wins.
